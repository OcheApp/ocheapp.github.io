Frequently Asked Questions

From the home screen, tap "Play" and select a game mode. Choose your players (or add AI opponents), configure the game settings, and tap "Start Game". You can play with 2–8 players depending on the mode.

In the game setup screen, tap the "+" button to add a player and select "Create AI Player". Choose a name, avatar, and ELO rating (400–2000). Higher ELO means a stronger opponent. AI players can be reused across games.

Pro unlocks all 7 training modes (free includes Score, Checkout, and Target Aim), career mode, tournaments, custom programs, achievements, AI coach, up to 32 players (free: 4), and the full AI ELO range up to 2000 (free: up to 1000).

One player hosts a game and receives a 6-character room code. Share this code with your opponent, who enters it to join. The connection is peer-to-peer when possible, with a relay fallback for reliability. Both players must be on the same game mode. The host configures all game settings.

During any game or training session, tap the microphone icon in the toolbar to switch to voice input. Hold the button and say your throw (e.g., "treble 20" or "double 16"). For hands-free mode, enable it in the voice settings — the app will listen continuously and submit throws automatically after detecting silence. Supported in English and Spanish.

Yes. Oche uses iCloud to sync your players, game history, statistics, and settings across all your Apple devices. Make sure iCloud is enabled for Oche in Settings > [Your Name] > iCloud > Apps Using iCloud.

You can delete individual players, games, or training sessions from within the app. To delete all data, uninstall the app from your device. If iCloud sync is enabled, you may also need to remove the app's data from iCloud via Settings > [Your Name] > iCloud > Manage Storage.
